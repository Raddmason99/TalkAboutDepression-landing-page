Prevent Learn Network About




501c 3 non profit organization building a platform to combat depression

Mission

Transforming the way we communicate with friends about mental health to save lives, decrease social stigma, and give unconditional support to those who need it most.

Vision

Individuals who experience challenges with their mental health deserve communities where they can belong and find hope. We provide a virtual knowledge platform and community for individuals and friends and families of individuals who experience such challenges.

Using the best communication channels and tools, we seek to empower individuals to handle high stakes situations as well as everyday life moments. We aim to transform the way our communities communicate with each other about mental health.
